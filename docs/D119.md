# 2025 Kick-off Bonus Financing Scheme (D119)

## 4. Eligibility
- Sales professionals from IFAs, Broker firms, FIs, etc.
- At least 2 years valid registration.
- AAI ≥ HK$500,000.

## 6. Kick-off Bonus (KOB)
- AAI capped at HK$1.92m.
- KOB = 25% of total MF (MF x 24).
- 100% paid upon contracting.

## 7. Monthly Finance (MF)
- MF amount: HK$10,000 – HK$80,000.
- Duration: Max 24 months.
- Validation: 325% of total MF in first 24 months.

## 8. Performance Bonus (PB)
- PB1 (1st-24th months): 25% or 50% of total MF.
- PB2 (1st-36th months): 25%, 50%, or 75% of total MF.

## 9. Financial Responsibility
- Repayment 100% if terminated within 36 months.
