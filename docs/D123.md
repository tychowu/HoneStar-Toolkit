# 2025 Professional Financing Scheme (D123)

## 4. Eligibility
- Master degree holder + 3 years experience.
- OR AAI ≥ HK$1,000,000.

## 6. Monthly Finance (MF)
- MF amount: HK$20,000 - HK$80,000.
- **Route 1**: HK$20,000 (Master + 3 yrs).
- **Route 2**: HK$30,000 (Master + AAI $750k).
- **Route 3**: HK$40k - $80k (AAI ≥ $1m).
- Duration: Max 24 months.

## 7. Performance Bonus (PB)
- Same structure as D119 (PB1 and PB2).

## 8. Financial Responsibility
- Within 24 months: 100% repayment.
- 25-36 months: 60% repayment.
- 37-48 months: 40% repayment.
