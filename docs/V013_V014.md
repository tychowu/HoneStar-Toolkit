# PRUVenture Elite Financing Scheme (Scheme Code: V013/V014)

## 1. Effective Period
- From 1 Jan 2023 – 31 Dec 2023.

## 2. Nature of Payment
- All financing payments (Monthly Finance, Performance Bonus, Promotion Bonus) are essentially loan payments.

## 5. Eligibility
| Scheme Type | V013 | V014 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Recruiting Manager** | SUM or up | SUM or up / Selective UM/FC/SFC/BSM/SBSM |
| **Entry Title** | SUM | FC/SFC/BSM/SBSM |
| **Monthly Finance** | HK$25k - $60k | HK$20,000 |
| **Basic Requirement** | 2 yrs exp + AAI ≥ $300k | Bachelor Degree OR 2 yrs exp |

## 7. Monthly Finance (MF)
- MF amount: HK$20,000 – HK$60,000.
- Payable for not more than 12 months.
- Validation: 350% of total MF in the first 12 months.

## 9. Performance Bonus (PB)
- Available in 13th to 24th months.
- Levels: 1st ($140k), 2nd ($210k), 3rd ($315k), 4th ($420k).
- Requirement: Net AFYP from $840k to $2.52m.

## 10. Promotion Bonus
- Promote to SUM: HK$10,000.
- Promote to BM: HK$50,000.
- Promote to RM/SRM: HK$200,000.
- Promote to RD/SRD: HK$500,000.
