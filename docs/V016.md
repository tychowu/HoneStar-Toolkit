# 2025 PRUVenture Allowance Scheme (V016)

## 1. Effective Period
- From 1 April – 31 December 2025.

## 4. Eligibility
- NOT eligible for KOB (D119) or Elite (V013/V014).
- Manager must be A+ Leaders Program graduate.

## 6. Monthly Allowance
- **Amount**: HK$10,000.
- **Duration**: Max 6 months.
- **Validation Rules**:
  - Monthly PRULeads score 200.
  - Net Annualized First Year Premium (NAFYP) requirement: 20,000/month.
  - Persistency ≥ 85%.
  - 1st month: 1 PRUDNA report.
  - 6th month: License IIQE II/IV/V and 10 training modules.

## 7. Financial Responsibility
- Pro-rata repayment if overall validation fails at 6th month.
- Full repayment if terminated within 36 months for joining competitors.
